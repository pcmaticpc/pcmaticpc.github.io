How to Access Your PC Matic Login Account

  1. Open your preferred web browser.
  2. Go to the official PC Matic website: https://www.pcmatic.com.
  3. Click on the Login option in the upper right corner.
  4. Enter your registered email address and password.
  5. Complete any security verification if prompted.
  6. Click Sign In to enter your dashboard.

For business accounts, the login process is the same, but you may see additional features related to group device management and policy controls.

Setting Up a New PC Matic Login Account

  1. Click on Login at the top of the PC Matic homepage.
  2. Select Create Account under the sign-in fields.
  3. Enter your email address, create a strong password, and fill in your account details.
  4. Verify your email by clicking the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  5. Once your account is created, you can download and activate PC Matic on your devices using the same credentials.

Navigating the PC Matic Dashboard

After logging into your pcmatic login account, you’ll see your account dashboard. For home users, this dashboard displays:

  • Your device list and their current security status.
  • Subscription renewal dates.
  • Scan results and history.
  • Quick links to run scans or update software.

For business users, the dashboard also includes:

  • Group policies for managing multiple devices.
  • Remote command capabilities.
  • Detailed security and performance analytics.

Resetting a Forgotten Password

  1. Go to the PC Matic login page.
  2. Click Forgot Password?.
  3. Enter your registered email address.
  4. Check your email for a password reset link.
  5. Follow the link to create a new password.

Remember to choose a secure password that includes uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Sometimes, you may face trouble logging in. Common solutions include:

  • Double-checking your email and password for typos.
  • Clearing your browser cache and cookies.
  • Disabling browser extensions that may block scripts.
  • Ensuring you’re on the official PC Matic site.
  • Resetting your password if necessary.

If problems persist, PC Matic’s support team can help verify your account details and restore access.

Security Best Practices for Your PC Matic Login Account
